Glide&Ride Site Plan

This is the name of the website of a company that rents snowboarding equipment

Site Purpose

This site helps ski lovers to choose the right ski equipment. We provide information about different types of snowboards and help to choose the right one.

Scenarios

  • What types of snowboards are there?

  • Which snowboard is best for me?

  • What are the advantages of different types of snowboards?
